Application Websites

Colleges of all calibors make you apply via application websites or their own college web browser. Familiarize yourself with all these websites and try to set up an account as quick as possible for it is the easiest step of them all. Signing up can take 5-10 minutes, however make sure you have a secure password for the information you put on application sites as they are very critical for your safety.

CommonApp

The CommonApp offers a wide array of colleges such as: CU Boulder, CSU, Harvard, Georgia Tech, Villanova, Cornell, Princeton, Dartmouth, Columbia, etc.

Coalition for College

Similar to the CommonApp, Coalition for College has a wide variety of colleges including: University of Washington, CalTech, Clemson, Duke, Purdue, Yale, University of Pennsylvania, etc.

ApplyTexas

Ironically, Texas decided to be different than all the other colleges. This website is mainly for Texas schools including: UT Austin, Texas Tech, University of Houston, Rice University, Texas A&M, etc.
