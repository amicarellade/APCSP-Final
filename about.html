Application Websites

Colleges of all calibors make you apply via application websites or their own college web browser. Familiarize yourself with all these websites and try to set up an account as quick as possible for it is the easiest step of them all. Signing up can take 5-10 minutes, however make sure you have a secure password for the information you put on application sites are very critical for your safety.
