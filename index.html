Welcome

For a lot of seniors out there, college planning is impossible. It seems as if colleges make it as complicated as possible to organize yourself due to all the different mediums they take to persuade you to come to their college. This website right here will help you organize all your colleges and help you not procrastinate the college application process as it is one of the biggest steps in your educational career.

School is stressful, and that is why we are here for you. Just follow all the steps and you'll be on your way to applying to your first colleges. Make sure to apply to colleges that YOU like. Here are some helpful hints:

  • Know the size of the college
  • Know the topography of the city
  • Tour the university
